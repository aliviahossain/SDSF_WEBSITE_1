Mandatory Admission Prerequisites

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet the following minimum academic requirements to be considered for admission:

  • Required Stream: Higher Secondary (10+2) with Science stream and Mathematics
  • General/OBC: At least 50% marks in aggregate or equivalent grade
  • SC/ST/DA: 45% marks in aggregate or equivalent grade
  • Entrance Test: Admission through CUET UG examination

Program Details

This program offers an integrated pathway to advanced technical roles, focusing on both software and analytical mastery.

  • Degree: Master of Technology (Dual Degree) in AI & Data Science
  • Duration: 5 Years Integrated Program (2024-29 structure)
  • Total Credits: 200+ credits across 10 semesters
  • Note: Course contents subject to modification per University Ordinance No. 14

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is this program equivalent to a standard B.Tech + M.Tech?

Yes, it integrates the undergraduate foundation and postgraduate specialization into a single, comprehensive 5-year dual degree program for accelerated qualification.

2. What is the required background in Mathematics?

Mathematics as a subject in the Higher Secondary (10+2) Science stream is a mandatory requirement for all applicants.

3. What is the final admission channel?

Admission is strictly processed based on the candidate's performance in the CUET UG entrance examination.

4. Are there internship opportunities during the program?

Yes, the program includes mandatory internships in Semester 6 (Internship-I) and Semester 8 (Internship-II), each carrying 4 credits to provide real-world industry experience.

5. What is the total credit requirement for graduation?

Students must complete over 200 credits across 10 semesters, including core courses, electives, projects, internships, and comprehensive viva-voce examinations.
